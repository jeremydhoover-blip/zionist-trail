# Zionist Trail

An Oregon Trail-style historical adventure game about the Jewish pioneer journey from Warsaw to Jerusalem in 1909.

## Play the Game

**[Play Zionist Trail](https://jeremydhoover-blip.github.io/zionist-trail/)**

## About

Lead a party of Jewish pioneers on the dangerous journey from Warsaw, Poland to Jerusalem. Travel by train across Europe, sail the Mediterranean Sea, and trek through Ottoman Palestine. Manage your resources, make daily decisions, and keep your party alive.

### The Route
Warsaw -> Krakow -> Vienna -> Trieste -> Mediterranean Sea -> Jaffa -> Tel Aviv -> Rishon LeZion -> Rehovot -> Hebron -> Bethlehem -> Jerusalem

### Features
- **Oregon Trail-style gameplay** with daily decisions, resource management, and random events
- **8 playable characters** with unique starting resources and abilities
- **Historical accuracy** based on the Second Aliyah period (1909)
- **100+ hand-crafted images** for locations, events, and characters
- **Dynamic weather system** with region-aware seasonal climate (continental Europe, Mediterranean Sea, semi-arid Palestine)
- **Calendar date system** starting March 15, 1909
- **Mini-games**: Mediterranean fishing and desert foraging
- **Party management**: 7 family members and companions with individual health, morale, and special abilities
- **Resource-based events**: Help or refuse strangers with your tools, food, medicine, water, and clothing

### How to Play
1. Choose your character profession
2. Select 5 traveling companions
3. Buy supplies at Goldstein's General Store in Warsaw
4. Make daily decisions: continue traveling, rest, trade, forage, fish, or explore
5. Manage your resources and keep your party healthy
6. Reach Jerusalem to win

## Development

Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step.

To run locally:
```
git clone https://github.com/jeremydhoover-blip/zionist-trail.git
cd zionist-trail
npx serve .
```

Or open index.html directly in a browser.
